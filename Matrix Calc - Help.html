Welcome To The Matrix Calculator

This is an online Matrix Calculator. You can do the following here:

  • Add two Matrices
  • Subtract two Matricies
  • Multiplication of 2 Matricies
  • Scalar Multiplication
  • Divide two Matricies
  • Get The Zero Matrix
  • Get the Identity Matrix
  • Get the Transpose of A
  • Get the Determinant of A
  • Get the Cofactor of a certain number in a Matrix
  • Get the Adjoint of a Matrix
  • Get the Inverse of a Matrix
  • Print your Matrix

Note: Items in bold are not completed. Please check back soon...

How to use it...

  1. Choose an Operation

    • Use the dropdown menu under the Title to pick your operation

  2. Put in your Matrix Dimensions

    • Type the dimensions of the Matrix in the input boxes under the Dropdown

    • You could also use the Sliders

    • For Scalar Multiplication,make sure the first matrix dimensions are One by One

  3. Hit Submit

    • Your matrices should show up below

    • Unnecessary Matrices and inputs are hidden automatically

  4. Put in your values

    • Type in the values in the boxes in the table

  5. Hit the Equal Sign

    • Your answer will show up below the button
