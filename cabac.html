Suanpan

Html, Jquery, JQM

The Chinese Abacus, also called the Suanpan consists of 10 rows with each row separated into two sections by a long bar. There are 5 beads on the bottom section and 2 at the top. The value of each of the top beads is 5 while the bottom beads are all valued at one. This allows for easier counting, while the school abacus solves this by using two different colors.
